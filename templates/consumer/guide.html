Consumer Play Guide

{% include "global/includes/help_msg.html" %}

Overview

The consumer player represents a large group of consumers. He has two goals (in order of importance):
  1. Make sure he has enough vehicles.
  2. Get the most attractive vehicles possible for his consumers.

Player Actions

The "Home" screen provides information about the player profile and fuel consumption.

Consumers buy vehicles on the "Global Screens -> Buy Vehicles" page.

{% include "global/includes/help_guide.html" %}

Strategy Hints

  • To maximize your score you need to meet your vehicle quota for the turn. Manage your vehicle purchases according to your budget.
  • Pay attention to the "Rank" column on the vehicle purchase screen (Global Screens -> Buy Vehicle). Low ranks indicate more attractive cars for your consumer, but do not use the ranks as your sole buying criteria.
  • Negotiate vehicle prices with the vehicle producers. Make them work for your money.
  • If your consumer profile has a "balance" interest you can earn points by not spending all of your money.
  • Fuel costs are deducted from your turn allowance at the beginning of the turn. High fuel costs decrease the level of vehicles you can buy in the turn.
