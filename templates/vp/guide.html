Vehicle Producer Play Guide

Overview

The goal of the vehicle producer (VP) is to maximize his profit. The VP makes money by selling his vehicles at a profit to consumers. The VP has two basic actions:
  • Creating vehicles to sell on the next game turn.
  • Investing RD points in order to improve the capabilities of the vehicles.

VP Game Actions

Vehicle producers build cars at [Private Screens -> Create Vehicle]. Building large runs of vehicles reduces your per unit cost. The minimum cost comes at a run of 250 vehicles. Beyond that the cost remains constant.

Vehicle producers improve and distinguish their vehicles by R and D (RD). Free RD points are awarded for when you make a profit on your vehicle. Area RD points are applied to your VP for producing and selling vehicles, based on the vehicle type sold.

  • For detailed information on RD point awards and how you earn them see [Private Screens -> R&D Awards].
  • To invest free points in RD areas of your choosing go to [Private Screens -> R & D Investment]

Get vehicle market information at [Global Screens -> Fleet Info]. More detailed market information is available as a csv file download for spreadsheet analysis in the link in green box at the end of page. The csv can be downloaded here.

Strategy Hints

  • Larger vehicle runs allow you to sell at lower prices.
  • Pay attention to the size of the market. Vehicles you don't sell are sold off at 75% of your build cost. Unsold vehicles can be costly.
  • You can negotiate special prices with consumers by using funds transfers. This allows you to sell a vehicle for less than official market price, without letting all consumers have the same price.
