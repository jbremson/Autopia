Glossary

A

B

Balance - 1) The player's account balance; 2) the frugality descriptor attribute for the consumer player. High balance scores indicate a frugal consumer.

BEV - Battery Electric Vehicle - a vehicle driven solely by electricity stored onboard in a battery.

C

CAFE - Corporate Average Fuel Economy - The fleet mpg a vehicle producer must reach in order to avoid a penalty fee. The penalty is based on the shortfall and the volume of sales of the vehicle producer.

Consumer - A player who buys vehicles and fuels.

D

Diesel - a fuel derived from crude oil and/or biomass.

Distress Sale - Vehicles sold off at a steep discount by the vehicle producer to clear inventory. Unsold vehicles are sold in this manner at the end of each turn into outside markets.

F

Fleet - A collection of vehicles. Each consumer in the game owns a 'fleet' of vehicles. Likewise the collection of all vehicles in Autopia is referred to as a 'fleet."

H

H2 - Hydrogen - abreviation for hydrogen. It can refer to hydrogen fuel or to a drivetrain that runs on hydrogen fuel.

HEV - Hybrid Electric Vehicle - A vehicle combining electric drive with another fuel type (i.e. gasoline, diesel, h2).
A HEV cannot be externally charged.

HFCV - Hydrogen Fuel Cell Vehicle - abreviation for hydrogen fuel cell vehicle

G

Gasoline - gas - a fuel derived from crude oil and/or biomass as ethanol.

M

Margin The percentage of gross revenue representing profit, e.g. a margin of 0.10 (10%) on a sale of $100 would mean $10 of the gross revenue was profit and $90 were cost related.

P

Performance - 1) an aggregate descriptor of the vehicle's power (i.e. hp), 2) a measure of the vehicle's ability to do work using the physics definition of 'work' (W=F*d).

PHEV - Plug-in Hybrid Vehicle - a HEV with a large battery that can be externally charged. PHEVs combine a
a short range (up to 40 miles) BEV drivetrain with a gas, diesel or hydrogen drivetrain. They offer the
efficiency of a BEV with the option of conventional refueling.

PHEV10 -Plug-in Hybrid Vehicle 10 - a PHEV with a 10 mile (estimated) battery only operation range.

PHEV40 -Plug-in Hybrid Vehicle 40 - a PHEV with a 40 mile (estimated) battery only operation range.

R

Road Load - The non-drivetrain related work of the vehicle. Includes electric load (e.g. lights, A/C), rolling efficiency and aerodynamics.

S

Style - an aggregate descriptor of the vehicle's presentation. It includes size, materials quality and the fit and finish of the vehicle.
