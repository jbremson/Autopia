Overview

The goal of the fuel producer is to maximize his profit over the course of the game. The fuel producer maximizes his profit by correctly predicting the movement of fuel demand over time.

The fuel producer has several primary game activities.

  • Manages refineries. Builds new refineries to account for refinery retirement. Activating, deactivating or eliminating them.
  • Buy and sell refineries amongst each other.
  • Build new refineries.

Strategies

  • If vehicle mpg increases the demand for fuel will fall.
  • Profit is maximized when fuel demand and supply are matched. Withholding productions and/or over-producing will reduce your profits.
  • Pay attention to your refinerys' remaining life statistic. Replace them in a timely and appropriate manner.
  • Deactivate surplus refineries to reduce operating costs and control supply.
  • Large refineries have better margins than small ones.
  • Refineries take time to build. The larger the refinery the longer the build time.
